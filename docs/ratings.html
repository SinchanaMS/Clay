Ratings

Ratings are provided by the customers or consumers who have either purchased a product or have had an experience of the service provided to them.
Usually, ratings are gauged using stars. Use class stars-containerin the parent container. Use class star along with the preferred fill options - filled-star, half-star or outlined-star.
